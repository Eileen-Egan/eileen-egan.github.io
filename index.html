My Projects

Corona Virus Adventure Game

My final project for Computer Science 200 was a Corona Virus adventure game. The premise of the game is to make your way through the city of Madison to find the Corona Virus vaccine. However along the way you have to be careful not to test postive for the virus, try to avoid college parties with large gatherings, etc. In the code I utilized JavaScript, and implmented techniques like If-Else Statements, and various different loop structures like Do, While, and For Loops, etc.
